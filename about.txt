About -

Fort destruction 3D is a fish simulation game.
Your goal is to thrive and prosper in the four walls of the fish-tank.
You can buy and sell fish, watch them, and even feed them.

This game an open source project, written in Java programing launguage by
Dror Sharf, and is submitted for the Matriculation in computer science.
The source code is avilable at - https://code.google.com/p/fortdestruction3d/source/list
